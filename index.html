Child Marriage in America

Child Marriage is an issue in America

Between 2000 and 2015, over 200,000 children were married in America. Over 50% of the children were girls. When you think of child marriage, you may think of girls being married in third-world and developing countries, but child marriage is a problem in America as well. Because of state laws allowing minors to marry with parental consent, many children are married before 18. Though child marriage rates in America are going down every year, child marriage will continue to happen in America without serious action.
