Child marriage is an extremely prevalent problem in America

Child Marriage:
A formal or informal union between a child under the age of eighteen and an adult or another child.

  
When you think of child marriage, you may think of girls being married in developing countries. But child marriage is a pressing matter in America. Though the yearly number of children married before the age of 18 decreases every year, child marriage won't cease without taking action.
